# AQOS Research Journal

> AI Quant Operating System (AQOS)
>
> This document serves as the centralized research journal for AQOS.
>
> It records academic papers, algorithms, mathematical concepts,
> trading methodologies, machine learning techniques, engineering ideas,
> experiments, benchmarks, and implementation decisions that influence
> the development of AQOS.
>
> This is a living document and will continuously evolve.

The objective of AQOS research is to build an institutional-grade AI Quant Research Platform by combining knowledge from:

- Quantitative Finance
- Machine Learning
- Deep Learning
- Reinforcement Learning
- Statistics
- Time Series Forecasting
- Market Microstructure
- Smart Money Concepts
- Artificial Intelligence
